## What is WatchTronome

WatchTronome is an Open Source LiteOS app for [Huawei Watch GT2 Pro](https://consumer.huawei.com/en/wearables/watch-gt2-pro/) which allows you to obtain a metronome that acts by making the watch vibrate.

## Where to download WatchTronome

WatchTronome is available in [Huawei Health app](https://consumer.huawei.com/en/mobileservices/health/).  
To download WatchTronome, you need to have a Huawei Watch GT2 Pro connected at your smartphone and click on AppGallery icon on the application.
